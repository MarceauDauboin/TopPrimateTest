ABOUT US

Top Primate Academy is a premier Muay Thai gym located centrally in the CBD of Cape Town. We offer high-level training and technical instruction in disciplines ranging from self-defense, to physical fitness, to combat sports. We are dedicated to providing you with the kind of quality training you deserve, and helping you develop physical fitness, self-confidence and mental toughness. True strength is forged under fire, and we believe in developing every single client, student and athlete in ways that help them to push past their fears and develop a 'never say die' attitude in every challenge they are faced with, both in the gym and in their everyday lives. Since its inception, Top Primate has catered to some of the very best Martial Arts Athletes, competing at the absolute highest levels of contest.



At Top Primate you are always surrounded by the best and the opportunities to learn and develop your fight game are endless. Whether you dream of becoming a World Champion, losing a couple kilograms, learning a new, useful, effective skill-set, providing a positive environment for your child to grow, or taking your own fitness & conditioning to the next level, you have come to the right place
